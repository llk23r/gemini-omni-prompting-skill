# storyboard_to_video

## Input

```
mode: storyboard_to_video
storyboard:
  Panel 1: Wide exterior. Empty concert hall entrance. Dawn light.
  Panel 2: Interior. Violinist entering the stage from the wings.
  Panel 3: Close-up on hands placing bow to strings.
  Panel 4: Medium shot of first bow stroke.
  Panel 5: Wide shot of stage from back of hall. Figure small against the space.
  Panel 6: Slow push-in to violinist's face. Eyes closed.
```

## Output

```
Shot 1: Wide exterior of a concert hall entrance at dawn. Static shot. 3s.
Shot 2: Interior. Violinist walks from stage wings to center. Medium wide. Slow dolly in. 4s.
Shot 3: Close-up on hands. Bow placed against strings. Static. 2s.
Shot 4: First bow stroke. Medium shot. Camera holds. 3s.
Shot 5: Wide from back of hall. Violinist at center. Locked-off. 4s.
Shot 6: Slow push-in from medium to close on face. Eyes closed. 5s.
```
